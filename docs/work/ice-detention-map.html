ICE Detention Facility Map

Mapping every U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement detention facility and its reported population, as of March 2026.

This dashboard maps all U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement detention facilities and their reported populations as of March 2026. It's built on an ArcGIS Dashboard, hosted separately from my other dulcetgis.com work, sourcing and structuring facility-level data that isn't otherwise easy to see in one place.

The technical goal was straightforward: take a large, dispersed federal system and make it visible and legible as a single map, rather than dozens of disconnected records.

Live dashboard, national coverage.
